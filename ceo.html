24 YEARS EXPERIENCE

Empowering Sustainability, Fuelling Success

CEO of AEPCOGROUP.COM

Throughout history, access to energy has been recognized as a fundamental human need and a pivotal driver of national economic growth. The Middle East region, with its abundant energy resources and vast reserves of oil, gas, and petrochemical products, has emerged as a prime area for conducting high-potential trades in these sectors.

Since our inception of ARABIAN ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION CO. in 1999, as part of introducing Innovative Environmental Solutions for a Greener Future we have continuously strived to offer cutting-edge solutions to address environmental challenges. In this regard, we introduced another groundbreaking initiative that cater to the specific needs of the oil and petrochemical industries, while also promoting a cleaner and more environmentally friendly operation. Hence, our next venture ARABIAN STAR OIL PROCESSING AND COLLECTION started which is dedicated to providing top-notch cleaning solutions for industrial and vessels' oil tanks. Our advanced processes not only guarantee efficient cleaning but also adhere to environmental standards, safeguarding the delicate marine ecosystems.

At ILINK PETROLEUM FZC we offer a wide range of high-quality petroleum products. Whether you require bunker fuels, lubricants, or other petroleum derivatives, our reliable supply chain ensures timely delivery to meet your demands. We envision the bigger picture and operate with a global mindset. We firmly believe in delivering unmatched services to our loyal customers, empowering their growth and success. Our commitment to providing high-quality products, exceptional performance, and punctuality has enabled us to expand our markets significantly and forge lasting partnerships worldwide.

Our core objective is to make a significant impact on the global market by offering tailored solutions that precisely respond to our customers' requirements and inquiries. To achieve this, we pride ourselves on staying ahead of the curve through continuous learning, employing cutting-edge knowledge, and providing the highest quality products. Our dedicated team of highly educated, experienced, and professional employees ensures that our offices uphold the standards of excellence that define our brand.

We invite you to join us in this endeavor by exploring the diverse range of services we now offer. Together, we can make a positive impact on our environment while contributing to the success and longevity of your operations.

Thank you

MANZOOR P MANIGAYIL

Managing Director & CEO
