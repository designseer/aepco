Mission Statement

At AEPCO GROUP, we are dedicated to safeguarding the environment, promoting industry excellence, and fostering sustainable growth. With a passionate commitment to responsible business practices, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we strive to set new standards in marine waste recycling and disposal services, Marine and oil industry cleaning solutions, and petroleum product sales.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as global leaders in marine waste recycling and disposal, oil industry tank cleaning services, and petroleum products sales. Through our unwavering commitment to our mission and core principles, we aspire to drive positive change within our industries and contribute to a greener, cleaner, and more prosperous future for generations to come.

Together, We Can Make a Difference

As we embark on this mission, let us remember that our collective efforts hold the power to effect real change. It is the dedication, creativity, and passion of each team member that will propel us toward success. Let's collaborate, inspire one another, and relentlessly pursue our goals with a sense of purpose.

Our Core Principles

  1. Stewardship: We understand the importance of preserving our oceans and marine ecosystems. Through cutting-edge technologies and responsible waste management practices, we aim to minimize the environmental impact of our operations and contribute to a cleaner, healthier planet.
  2. Industry Excellence: Our team of experts is driven by a pursuit of excellence in every facet of our services. We continuously invest in research, development, and training to provide state-of-the-art solutions that meet and exceed industry standards.
  3. Innovation: We embrace innovation as a catalyst for positive change. By pushing the boundaries of what's possible, we endeavor to develop new methodologies and technologies that revolutionize the sectors we operate in.
  4. Customer-Centric Approach: Our customers' needs and satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. We aim to build lasting partnerships by delivering tailor-made solutions, exceptional service, and unparalleled value.
  5. Sustainable Growth: We believe that growth should not come at the expense of the environment or societal well-being. By aligning our business strategies with sustainable practices, we seek growth that benefits both our stakeholders and the world around us.

I would like to reaffirm and celebrate our unwavering commitment to the pillars that define who we are as an organization: our valued customers, the environment we share, and the unity we foster among our team.
